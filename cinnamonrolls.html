I n g r e d i e n t s

  • 1½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1¼ tsp. Diamond Crystal or ¾ tsp. Morton kosher salt
  • ¾ tsp. baking soda
  • ¾ cup (1½ sticks) unsalted butter
  • 1 cup (200 g) dark brown sugar
  • ¼ cup (50 g) granulated sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 large egg yolks
  • 2 tsp. vanilla extract
  • 6 oz. bittersweet chocolate or semisweet chocolate chips

I n s t r u c t i o n s

  1. Preheat oven to 375°
  2. Whisk flour, salt, and baking soda in a bowl
  3. Cook 1 stick of butter in saucepan over medium heat. Swirl and scrape with rubber spatula until foams and browns, approx. 4 minutes.
  4. Let butter cool for 1 minute and add remaining 1/2 stick to it stirring until melted
  5. Add all sugar to butter, whisking and breaking up any clumps. Once fully incorporated, add egg and yolks, followed by vanilla.
  6. Combine wet and dry ingredients together, folding with spatula until not dry spots remain.
  7. Add chocolate chips until evenly distributed
  8. Spoon onto baking sheet and cook for approx. 8-10 minutes or until golden brown around the edges.
